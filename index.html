Introduction

An avid Gamer and Games Programming (BSc Hons) Student at Staffordshire University.

From birth, I was always surrounded by games, whether it was board games, or the PS1 & Original Xbox, there was always something to do. This exposure allowed me to rapidly grow knowledge about the world of gaming and games programming. I have a vast interest in a variety of games, such as League of Legends, Destiny 2, and the Call of Duty Franchise.

This interest pushed me down the career path of Games Programming, and thus I signed up for Staffordshire University to ensure a future in this field. In this portfolio you will find some of my projects, as well as a list of skill that I have developed at University.

Portfolio

In this section, you will find a variety of my previous and current projects, as well as anything that I have participated in that I am proud of.

Pacman

My current University project is a pacman game. We were tasked to create pacman, but also advised to put our own twist on the game. Later down the line, I will revisit this project and hope to add a few more powerups to the game, like a Munchie Magnet (has a nice ring to it, don't you think?)

UKIE Game Jam

Not too long ago, me and my group (collectively known as "Bethesdn't") participated in the UKIE Game Jam. We were challenged to create a game to fit the theme of diversity and inclusiveness. For this we decided on a puzzle game, in which you control 3 different coloured Wizards that could open doors based on their colours, you can also mix the orbs to create colours, such as magenta and cyan. You must utilise all 3 colours to pass each level.

University Game Jam

During our first week of University, we were told to get into teams, and create a game. So me and the rest of Team Bethesdn't got to work on a Skyrim-themed First Person endless runner game. We were able to get 3rd place with this game, and we got to have a little bit of fun while creating the game, making the whole experience a very memorable one.
